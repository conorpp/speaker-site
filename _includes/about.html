About

The Modular Speaker cabinets were designed by a group of audio-passionate students at Virginia Tech. The idea was to make a simple speaker that just works for just about any application. Just plug it in and it can be used in a theater, desktop, living room, local business, or studio. And it will sound great.

Equivalent speaker systems on the market can get complicated and cost in the thousands. Since we produce everything and designed it to be easily assembled, you can get two "modular," studio-quality speaker setup for just $500.
